Code of Conduct and Institutional Policies

Code Of Conduct for Students

  1. Each student in our institution signs an undertaking to uphold the following codes at the time of admission.
  2. All students are required to carry their valid identity cards to the college/clinical.
  3. Students who do not have ID cards will not be allowed on campus.
  4. All are expected to behave courteously and fairly with everyone, inside & outside the college.
  5. Students must arrive in the classroom five minutes earlier each day.
  6. Ragging in any form is banned in the college, hostel, clinical area, sports ground and canteen.
  7. Follow dress code.
  8. Respect the faculty.
  9. Be a good role model to junior students.
  10. Maintain silence in class, library and corridors.
  11. Take care of college and hospital property.
  12. Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited.
  13. Students must attend classes and clinical regularly.
  14. No leave without approval of HOD or relevant staff.
  15. Changes in address must be reported to the principal.
  16. Students cannot arrange meetings or excursions without principal approval.

Dress Code

Boys

  1. Wear formal pants and shirts.
  2. T-shirts and jeans are not allowed.
  3. Wear black formal shoes only.
  4. Maintain neat hair and beard.
  5. Wear clean ironed uniform coat.
  6. Shirt should be tucked in properly.

Girls

  1. Neatly ironed coat with clean uniform.
  2. Small ear stud only permitted.
  3. Only chudithar and salwar with dupatta allowed.
  4. Jeans and short chudithar not allowed.
  5. Hair neatly tied with black clips.
  6. Nails short, no nail polish.
  7. No jewellery in clinical areas.
  8. Black cut shoes in clinical.
  9. No hair colouring.

Anti-Ragging Rules

Administrative Action

  1. Students guilty of ragging will be punished as per regulations.
  2. Anti-ragging committee will determine punishment.
  3. Punishments may include:
  4. Suspension from classes.
  5. Withdrawal of scholarship.
  6. Debarring from examinations.
  7. Withholding results.
  8. Debarring from representing institution.
  9. Suspension from hostel.
  10. Cancellation of admission.
  11. Rustication.
  12. Expulsion from institution.

Code Of Conduct for the Principal

  1. Recognize common institutional objectives.
  2. Treat all employees equally.
  3. Prevent sexual harassment.
  4. Maintain financial transparency.
  5. Promote professional behaviour.
  6. Encourage faculty conferences and workshops.
  7. Encourage research publications.
  8. Support extracurricular activities.
  9. Promote decentralization.
  10. Maintain healthy academic atmosphere.

Code Of Conduct for the Faculty Member

  1. Educate students and encourage learning.
  2. Support weaker students.
  3. Accept criticism.
  4. Treat all students equally.
  5. Act as role models.
  6. Instill civic duty and patriotism.
  7. Maintain professional dignity.
  8. Ensure strong medical science foundation.
  9. Stay updated with medical innovations.
  10. Encourage research.
  11. Mentor seminars and activities.
  12. Support student well-being.
  13. Interact with parents in PTM.
  14. Follow professional ethics.
  15. Follow research principles.
  16. Provide high quality patient care.
  17. Respect colleagues and staff.
  18. Avoid conflicts with private practice.
  19. Maintain regularity.

Code Of Conduct for Support Staff

  1. Follow institutional policies.
  2. Inform authorities before leave.
  3. Do not engage in other work during duty hours.
  4. Avoid political activities.
  5. Treat everyone with respect.
  6. Maintain punctuality.
  7. Handle equipment carefully.
  8. Avoid drugs, tobacco or alcohol.
  9. Maintain confidentiality.
  10. Do not falsify documents.
  11. Maintain honesty.
  12. Avoid emotional relations with students.
  13. Respect management decisions.
  14. Resolve conflicts amicably.
  15. Maintain harmony within campus.

Hostel Rules

  1. Students must submit hostel admission form.
  2. Follow mess timings strictly.
  3. Rooms allotted will not be changed normally.
  4. Roll call mandatory at 7am and 7pm.
  5. Keep rooms and surroundings clean.
  6. Students must return before 7pm.
  7. Weekend leave requires permission.
  8. Study hours 8.30pm to 10.30pm.
  9. Switch off fans and lights.
  10. Visitors must register in visitor book.
  11. Sickness must be reported to warden.
  12. Students must obtain sick leave permission.
  13. No overnight stay outside hostel.
  14. Visitors must have pass.
  15. No visitors allowed inside rooms.
  16. Students must leave hostel before classes.
  17. No student allowed to stay during college hours.
  18. Avoid bringing valuables.
  19. Boys not allowed in girls hostel.
  20. Ragging prohibited.
  21. No celebrations or parties.
  22. Parents must bring visitor pass.
  23. Avoid late reporting.
  24. Damage to hostel property must be paid.
  25. Avoid using cell phones after hours.
  26. Narcotic drugs strictly prohibited.
  27. No anti-social activities.
  28. Unauthorized guests prohibited.
  29. Electrical appliances prohibited.
  30. Terrace access not allowed.
