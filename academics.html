Courses Offered

  • DGNM
  • B.Sc. Nursing
  • M.Sc. Nursing

DGNM

Programme Duration

3 Years Programme

Admission Criteria

  • Age: 17 years on or before December 31
  • Qualification: 10+2 with English and minimum 40% marks in qualifying examination from recognized board
  • Gender: Only Females

B.Sc. Nursing

Programme Duration

4 Years Programme

Admission Criteria

  • Age: 17 years on or before December 31
  • Qualification: 10+2 with Science (PCB) & English with minimum 45% marks from AISSCE / CBSE / ICSE / SSCE / HSCE or equivalent board
  • Gender: Both Male and Female

M.Sc. Nursing

Programme Duration

2 Years Programme

Clinical Specialities

  • Medical Surgical Nursing 5 Seats
  • Obstetric & Gynaecological Nursing 2 Seats
  • Paediatric (Child Health) Nursing 3 Seats
  • Psychiatric (Mental Health) Nursing 2 Seats
  • Community Health Nursing 2 Seats

Admission Criteria

  1. Candidate should be a Registered Nurse and Registered Midwife or equivalent.
  2. Minimum qualification: B.Sc Nursing / B.Sc Hons Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc Nursing with minimum 55% aggregate.
  3. Candidate should have completed the required nursing degree programme.
  4. Minimum one year work experience after Basic B.Sc Nursing.
  5. Minimum one year work experience prior or after Post Basic B.Sc Nursing.
